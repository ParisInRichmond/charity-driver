# 🎬 TikTok Video #001 — "I Built This for Oakland"
**Type:** Serious / Inspirational / Founder Story
**Target:** Viral storytelling — community + startup niche
**Hook style:** POV + emotional reveal
**Length:** 60-90 seconds
**Estimated viral potential:** 🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥 (very high — founder story + community angle + Oakland)

---

## 📱 SCRIPT

**[ON CAMERA — you, phone held close, eye contact, real energy. Start mid-action, not staged.]**

---

**[SECOND 0-3 — HOOK. Say this fast, with weight:]**
> "I grew up in East Oakland watching people sleep on the streets every single day."

**[Cut or pause — let it land]**

---

**[SECOND 4-15 — THE PROBLEM:]**
> "And I always thought — someone should do something about this.
> Then one day I realized...
> that someone was me."

**[Pull up your phone / laptop showing parisirving.com]**

---

**[SECOND 16-35 — THE REVEAL:]**
> "So I built Charity Driver.
> It's a platform that shows you — in real time — where unhoused people need help in Oakland.
> You can see exactly what they need. Food, water, blankets.
> You drive there. You deliver it. You log it.
> And the map updates so no one gets missed."

**[Screen record showing the live map with glowing gold pins]**

---

**[SECOND 36-50 — THE EMOTION:]**
> "This isn't an app.
> This is what happens when someone who grew up in the hood
> decides to use technology
> not to get rich —
> but to actually give back."

**[Hold eye contact. Don't blink. This is your money moment.]**

---

**[SECOND 51-65 — THE CALL TO ACTION:]**
> "The site is live right now at parisirving.com.
> If you're in the Bay — come help.
> If you're a nonprofit or investor — let's talk.
> Because we help not because we have to.
> We help because we WANT to."

**[End with logo/website on screen. No outro music needed — silence hits harder.]**

- Film in portrait mode (9:16)
- Natural lighting, outdoors in Oakland if possible — Lake Merritt, Fruitvale, Downtown backdrop
- No filter needed — raw is better for this type of content
- Show the actual website on screen (screen record separately, overlay in edit)
- Use CapCut: add subtitles auto, trending melancholic/inspirational beat underneath at low volume
- Hook in first 3 seconds is everything — if they scroll past, you lost them
