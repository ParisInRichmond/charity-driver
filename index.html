The Reality
Behind every statistic is a person.
A name. A story. A neighbor.

Oakland has over 4,800 unhoused residents. They are not invisible — they are part of our community. Charity Driver exists so that ordinary people can show up for them, in real time, with real resources.

Why We Exist
"The greatest act is to give
when it costs you something."

Charity Driver was born in Oakland — a city full of brilliance, resilience, and real need. We believe that when a person chooses to lay something down for a stranger — their time, their resources, their comfort — that's not charity. That's love. And love is what changes cities.
