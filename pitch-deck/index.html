The Problem
Oakland has 4,800+ unhoused neighbors.
Coordination is the missing piece.
4,800+
Unhoused individuals in Oakland
$1B+
CA annual homeless funding — yet numbers grow
0
Real-time donor coordination tools exist
Donors want to help but don't know where to go, what's needed, or whether someone has already been served today. Existing systems are government-facing, slow, and inaccessible to community volunteers.
The Solution
Charity Driver — Show up smarter.
📍

Real-Time Oakland Map

10 hotspot zones with color-coded urgency — red to green — updating live as donors submit deliveries.

🚗

Delivery Logging System

Donors log what they brought, where, and how many people served. Live feed updates instantly — no duplicate efforts.

🧑

Individual Support Profiles

Private, consent-based profiles so donors can recognize and consistently serve the same individuals over time.

🤖

Mia — AI Community Guide

Warm, human chatbot that guides donors through the platform and answers questions in real time.

Our Mission
