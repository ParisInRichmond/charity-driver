## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Charity Driver is a technology platform that connects free-roam donors with unhoused individuals across Oakland, California. The platform uses real-time mapping, community-driven delivery logging, and an AI-assisted chatbot to coordinate volunteer efforts and track community impact.

Founded by Paris Irving — Innovation Educator, Hidden Genius Project alumnus, and Oakland native — Charity Driver was built on a single belief: that ordinary people, choosing to show up with food, water, or clothing, can do what institutions cannot. They can make someone feel seen.

**The problem:** Oakland has 4,800+ unhoused residents. Existing systems are fragmented, slow, and lack real-time coordination tools for community donors.

**The solution:** A free platform where donors can see where help is needed right now, log deliveries, and build ongoing relationships with the individuals they serve — all in real time.

**Revenue model:** Freemium subscription ($7/month Supporter tier), government/nonprofit partnerships, grant funding, and future corporate sponsorships.

**Funding requested:** $25,000–$50,000 (seed/grant stage)

## THE PROBLEM

Oakland consistently ranks among the top 5 cities in the US for homelessness per capita. Despite billions in government spending, the unhoused population continues to grow. The gap is not money — it's coordination.

Community donors want to help but don't know:
- Where to go
- What is most needed
- Whether someone has already been served today
- How to find the same individuals they've helped before

Existing apps are either government-facing (inaccessible to community donors) or donation portals (no real-time field coordination).

---

## THE SOLUTION

Charity Driver fills the coordination gap with four core features:

**1. Real-Time Oakland Map**
10 high-need hotspot zones mapped across Oakland with color-coded urgency indicators (red = high need, green = recently served). Updates in real time as donors submit deliveries.

**2. Delivery Logging System**
Donors select location, resources delivered, and people served. Every submission updates the live feed instantly — eliminating duplicate efforts and showing where help is still needed.

**3. Individual Support Profiles** (Subscriber feature)
Private, consent-based profiles for individuals donors have met in the field. Uses first name or alias only. Allows consistent, intentional support over time.

**4. Mia — AI Community Guide**
Warm, human chatbot that guides new users through the platform, answers questions about the mission, and helps donors take action immediately.
