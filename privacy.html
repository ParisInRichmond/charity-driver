Privacy, Consent & Data

Charity Driver is built on trust. This page explains exactly how we handle data — for donors, users, and especially for the individuals we serve.

🧡 The short version: We collect only what we need. We never sell data. Profiles of unhoused individuals require consent. You can delete your account anytime.

What data we collect

  • Account info: name, email, password (encrypted), role, city
  • Delivery logs: location, resources delivered, people served, notes
  • Individual profiles: first name or alias only, zone, needs, interaction notes (consent required)
  • Chatbot interactions: questions asked (used to improve Mia)

What we never collect

  • Full legal names of unhoused individuals
  • Exact GPS locations of encampments
  • Medical diagnoses or sensitive personal health information
  • Payment card data (handled entirely by Stripe)

Profile consent policy

Individual support profiles may only be created:

  • With the verbal consent of the individual being profiled
  • By verified volunteers or partner organizations
  • Using only a first name or chosen alias — never a full legal name

Profiles are private and accessible only to logged-in subscribers. They are never public and never indexed by search engines.

How we use your data

  • To power your donor dashboard and delivery history
  • To improve Mia's responses based on common questions
  • To update the live urgency feed when deliveries are submitted
  • To send account-related emails (confirmation, password reset)

We do not sell, share, or monetize your data. Ever.

Your rights

  • You can delete your account at any time from your dashboard
  • You can request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • You can request deletion of any profile you created

Contact

Privacy questions? Reach us at our contact page. We respond within 24 hours.
